    The Balancing Boric
    Acid Suppository



    The vaginal suppository that combines Boric Acid, Lactic Acid, and
    Vitamin C to promote pH balance.


    1

    Wash your hands and vulva/bikini area with a mild, fragrance
    free soap.


    2

    Hold the suppository with your thumb and forefinger and insert
    it into your vagina, as far up as comfortably possible. Some
    people find that lying on their back with their knees bent is helpful.


    3

    The suppository will begin to dissolve within a few minutes.Insert
    1 suppository per day, every day for 10 days.
